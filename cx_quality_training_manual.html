What is Quality Assurance?

QA is the process of monitoring and analyzing phone calls, emails, messages, and customer conversations — ensuring all interactions meet the established standard.

🎧
Monitor
Listen to calls, review chats, audit CRM entries
📊
Analyze
Score against standard criteria, identify gaps
📈
Improve
Coach agents, calibrate standards, drive quality up
💡
Key Skills for QA: Active listening, attention to detail, objectivity, coaching ability, product knowledge, and report writing.

SMS Response Management
Communication Standards

Effective management of SMS communications is crucial for ensuring a positive customer experience. This covers how to appropriately respond to SMS messages received from clients, maintaining professionalism and efficiency.

Objectives of SMS Response Management
Provide timely and accurate responses to client inquiries or feedback
Ensure consistency and clarity in communication
Address customer concerns and maintain high service quality
SMS Drive
Sending targeted messages to prompt specific customer actions, responses, or information
Used for campaigns, surveys, and outbound communication initiatives
📱
Best Practices: Respond within SLA timeframe · Use the customer's language · Proofread before sending · Maintain professional tone · Follow standard templates where applicable · Always include agent name/identifier.
