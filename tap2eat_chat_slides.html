Common Mistakes That Cost Quality Points

💀
Dropping the parent
"I will check and get back to you" — then radio silence. Always give a timeframe and follow up.
😤
Defensive or dismissive
"That's not our fault" / "You should have checked" — never blame the parent. Own the issue.
📝
SMS-style shorthand
"pls send details", "k", "ok sori" — write in full sentences. You're not texting a friend.
🔇
No closure or confirmation
Ending without confirming the parent is satisfied. Always ask "Is there anything else I can help with?"
